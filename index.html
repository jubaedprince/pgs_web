About

Get ready to experience a new breed of mobile cricket, unlike anything you’ve ever seen before.Presenting the next product from the Cricket Career series - Cricket Career: Super League. For the first time in the history of mobile cricketing, such a cricket game is being brought to existence by Pechas Game Studios; an indie game development team based in the heart of Bangladesh.


Cricket Career Super League will be the first-of-its-kind management-action hybrid game where the user can play as the manager of a team and get the chance to enjoy the thrill of an adrenaline filled, supercharged batting experience through a tap-swipe batting game!


Train your players, Trade your players, conduct PR activities, and sell Merchandise to become the top team in the world. Only you can lead your team to the apex of this Cricket Career! The only place where fantasy and action meets cricket - Cricket Career Super League!

About PGS

PGS Logo
